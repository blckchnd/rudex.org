The DEX

The DEX is a one of a kind decentralized exchange, which is like a swiss knife — has all the functions you need and a couple of those you didn’t know it has. Despite its youth, with a blockchain technology in its core BitShares can operate the volume comparable to NASDAQ and still able to place your orders next second you submitted them.

Centralized exchanges are an old habit, decentralized — the future, which is already here.

Advantages of a decentralized exchange

  • protection from abuse by other individuals and governments (e.g. bail-in and bail-out secure)
  • transfers to any destination in the world in 3 seconds
  • guaranteed private money, uncontrollable by the central banks
  • freedom of trading between any types of assets in a unified international market
  • the possibility of micropayments
  • a clear logical structure that is easy to use
  • Anonymity
  • Decentralization
